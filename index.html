Mana Prema Prayanam

"Kshanam oka yugamla anipisthundi nuvvu pakkana lekunte...
aa yuganni kshanamla marchesi naa lokam nuvve kavali."

Naa Banagaram


Day 1 • Rose Day
, ee rose lo unna parimalam kante, mana parichayam icchina gnapakaale ekkuva rojulu gurtuntay.
Day 2 • Propose Day
, naa prathi oopiri nee gnapakam thone modhalavali... ee deham katta kale varaku nee cheyi viduvani oka thodu la, naa lokam nuvve avthava?
Day 3 • Chocolate Day
Chocolate kante sweet ga mana kaburlu unte, inka ee teepi enduku? Mana prema eppatiki ilaage thiyyaga undali.
Day 4 • Teddy Day
Nenu lenappudu ee teddy-ey nee thodu. Nee prema aina, kopam aina denithone panchuko... nee prema kevalam naaku, ee teddy-ki mathrame chandhaali!
Day 5 • Promise Day
Govindudi saksiga chepthunna... kaalam tho paatu karigipoye maata kaadu idi, naa gundetho paatu aagipoye varaku unde nijam.
Day 6 • Hug Day
Ninnu daggara teesukunnappudu aagipoye aa kshanam... prapancham lo unna anni badhalani marchipoye magic undi.
Day 7 • Kiss Day
Mana mounam, ee challani gaali... ee kshanam mana prema kathalo eppatiki oka madhura gnapakamai migilipovaali.
Day 8 • Valentine's Day
Arundhati Nakshatram laaga, mana bandham nithya sathyam. Janma janmala ee prayanam neethone, neekosame!
The Final Question
"Prema aney ee prapancham lo, nadhi anukonedhi anukoney oka pilla nuvvu avutava?"
